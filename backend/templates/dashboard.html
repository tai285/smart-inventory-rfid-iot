Tag State Reference

click to expand ▾

Legacy cycle (1-reader demo): out → in → consumed → return_pending → in → … (scan confirms return and restores to warehouse in one step)

outTag registered; item not yet received into stock.
inItem in warehouse / active stock. Next scan will dispatch it.
consumedItem dispatched / used up. Tag inactive until admin requests return.
return pendingAdmin requested return. Stock unchanged until physical scan confirms.
returnedPipeline mode only: item back at warehouse dock, awaiting racking or dispatch.
taggedNew tag written at factory (pipeline mode).
in transitLeft factory floor, en route to warehouse (pipeline mode).
receivedArrived at warehouse dock, awaiting racking (pipeline mode).
rackedPlaced on warehouse shelf at a specific rack location.
dispatchedSent to customer. Terminal state unless returned.
